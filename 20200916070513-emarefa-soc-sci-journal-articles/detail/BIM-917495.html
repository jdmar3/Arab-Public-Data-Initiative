The aim of the research is to identify the impact of strategic intelligence through its dimensions (vision, vision and motivation) in achieving marketing flexibility through its dimensions (distribution, promotion, pricing and product). The research sample included employees of Al Fahad. A questionnaire was used as a main source in the data collection.
