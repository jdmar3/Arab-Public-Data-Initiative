Abstract EN

The research has aimed at identifying the impact of elements of the learning organization (team learning, systemic thinking, self-mastery of employees, organization environment) in achieving the competitive advantage with its dimensions (quality, cost reduction, innovation). The research population has consisted of all employees of Orange Jordan Telecommunication. The research sample has consisted of (156) employees of the company.
